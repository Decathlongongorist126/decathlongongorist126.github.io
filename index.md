## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I recover files from a formatted drive?
Yes. This application handles drives that show as empty after a format.

### Does this work on external hard drives?
Yes. You can plug in your external drive or USB stick and select it from the main menu.

### Will this software fix a physically broken drive?
No. This tool only handles software-level issues. If your computer does not see the drive at all, the hardware might be damaged. 

### Why should I save files to a different drive?
When you delete a file, the computer marks that space as available for new data. If you write new files to that space, you will overwrite the old data. Saving to a different drive keeps your lost data safe during the recovery process.
